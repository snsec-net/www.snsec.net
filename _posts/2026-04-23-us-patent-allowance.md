Our patent, "Lightweight Real-Time Abnormality Detection Method Using CAN Message Analysis and Neural Network Model" (Application No. 18/715,263), has received a Notice of Allowance from the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO). This patent covers a lightweight neural network-based approach for real-time intrusion detection on in-vehicle CAN bus networks.
