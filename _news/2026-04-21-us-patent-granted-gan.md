Our patent, "Generative Adversarial Network Model and Training Method to Generate Message ID Sequence on Unmanned Moving Objects" (US **Reg. No. 12,608,603**), has been **granted** by the USPTO. The work proposes a GAN-based approach for synthesizing realistic CAN message ID sequences in unmanned moving objects.
